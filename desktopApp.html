Scheduling — time saved
  • CAO / dispatcher: ~30–45 min per day building and sending the schedule.
  • PMs: another ~10–15 min re-explaining where people were scheduled and why.
  • Field: 5–10 min of “where am I again / where’s the address / what tools?” per person.

This board centralizes all of that — schedule, addresses, JHAs, tool assignments — and generates the messaging. The combined savings is easily 8–10 hours per week company-wide.

Field Ops story

Right now, tools, JHAs, and daily production live in separate places (or not at all). When something goes wrong — a near-miss, an upset client, missing tools — someone has to reconstruct the night from memory.

This screen means:

  • Every critical job has a JHA tied to it, with signatures and controls selected.
  • Every tool move is a quick tap, building a history you can actually trust.
  • Daily “goal vs actual” logs roll up to PM / Exec dashboards and change orders.

API / integration story (for CAO / CEO)

The point isn’t to replace Teams, Knowify, or Bamboo — it’s to make them feel like one system that works the way 48 Electric actually runs jobs.

  • Teams: this app publishes schedules, JHAs, and training reminders into the channels you already use.
  • Knowify: job numbers and budgets come from Knowify; this app sends back updated time, OT forecasts, and blockers.
  • BambooHR: remains the people system; this app adds field-skills and job-specific readiness for data center work.
  • DCIM / BMS: gives change windows and alarms; this app applies that to where you send crews and what MOPs / JHAs are required.
